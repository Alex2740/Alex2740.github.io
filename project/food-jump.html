Food Jump

Project Status Released Project Type Gamejam Project Duration 1 week Software Used Unity, Aseprite Languages Used C#

About Food Jump

Food Jump is the first game I made by myself. It was created during the BlackthornProd Game Jam, a one-week game jam. I worked on it in the evenings after school. The theme was Less is more.

First version

With the theme Less is more, I decided to go for a platformer with an extra mechanic. I thought of a Sonic fan game where Sonic became fat when he collected rings. So I decided to play a game where you have to collect all the food in the level. Food makes you fat and slows down your movements. The only way to lose weight is to run.

I then created the visuals and music for the game and started to create the game with unity.

The game finished 1st in the Theme: Category and received about 10 comments. People generally liked the concept of the game but the weight loss mechanics were frustrating. The weight loss was too slow.

Updated version

Following the feedback and after the render, I decided to revise the weight loss mechanics to make the game less frustrating. I chose to change the mechanics to weight loss when the player picks up a certain food. The player gains weight when eating fatty food and loses weight when eating fruit or vegetables. The levels then became puzzles because the food must be eaten in the right order.
